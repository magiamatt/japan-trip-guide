🚇 Helpful Japanese Phrases

Transportation

Eki wa doko desu ka? — Where is the station?

Kono basu wa ___ ni ikimasu ka? — Does this bus go to ___?

___ made ikura desu ka? — How much to ___?

Hotels & Check-In

Yoyaku shiteimasu. — I have a reservation.

Chekku in o onegaishimasu. — I'd like to check in, please.

Muryou Wi-Fi wa arimasu ka? — Is there free Wi-Fi?

Everyday Use

Arigatou gozaimasu. — Thank you very much.

Toire wa doko desu ka? — Where is the restroom?

Eigo o hanasemasu ka? — Do you speak English?

💡 Tips for Travelers

  • Save locations and directions to Google Maps for offline use.
  • Carry a pocket Wi-Fi or Japanese SIM card for smooth navigation.
  • Use apps like Google Translate for on-the-go help.
  • Screenshot hotel addresses in Japanese to show locals if needed.
